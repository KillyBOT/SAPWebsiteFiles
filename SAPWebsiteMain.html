What is this?

Hello! My name is Kyle. I am an eighth grade student at The School At Columbia. This website was made as a social action project against the War on Science. For the uninitiated, the War on Science is the battle between Scientists and people defunding and inhibiting scientific research. Many scientists have been forced to churn out scientific reports that have catchy names rather than research that could benefit our society. For instance, according to this pie chart on the National Priorities Project, less than one percent of national spending goes to science.

This is a major problem! Science is something that is needed throughout the world, and limiting scientific growth is the equivalent to tying a hand behind your back. Science is how we move forward, how we cure cancer, and how we save the world!

This webiste aims to somehow spread scientific understanding and give science the appreciation it needs. On this website, you will find a plethora of cool science things including cool stories from scientists, books you can read, online science stories and other interesting things. Hopefully, this will help at least a little by spreading the love of science, and in turn helping save it.
